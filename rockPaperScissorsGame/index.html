Rock-Paper-Scissor
Game

Instructions:

You have to choose one of these options each turn:

The option 1 stands for rock, option 2 for scissors, and the option 3 for paper.

The computer will then select a random option.

A winner will be determined by comparing your choice to the computer's choice.

Guidelines for Scoring:

  • The rock win over the scissors (because the rock smashes the scissors)
  • The scissors win over the paper (because the scissors cut the paper)
  • The paper win over the rock (because the paper covers the rock)
